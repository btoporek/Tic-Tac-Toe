Welcome to the game of Tic-Tac-Toe!

Directions: The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. You are X and your friend is O. Players take turns clicking an empty box to make their mark (X or O). The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. If all 9 squares are full and no one has 3 marks in a row, the game is a draw.
